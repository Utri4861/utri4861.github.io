Pop Mechanics: 24-Pounder Cannon

CAD / Fusion 360

Props & Models Complete
Fusion 360 CAD 3D Modeling

Cannon

The plans for this cannon were pulled from a vintage Popular Mechanics book I found at the thrift store for 25¢. I had just found the book when I started my summer Fusion 360 course, so I was hungry for any sort of plans to practice with. And of course, what's better than a massive ship cannon?

Originally supposed to be turned on a lathe with the carriage cut out of wood, it proved a wonderful early exercise in Fusion that informed some of my early design choices in coursework.
