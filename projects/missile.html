Design: AIM-9 Sidewinder

CAD and print exercise

Fusion 360 3D Printing Complete
Fusion 360 CAD 3D Printing Tolerance Fitting

This side project started as a quick CAD exercise inspired by missile forms and ended up being especially satisfying because it worked the first time. No test-print spiral, no redesign loop, just a clean one-and-done build.

A tolerance exercise that stayed fun

I approached the model as a way to practice fit and assembly while still making something visually striking. It was a reminder that even a small project can be rewarding when the geometry, print setup, and assembly choices line up cleanly.
