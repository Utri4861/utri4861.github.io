Helldivers 2: B-01 Armor

Video game costume

3D Printing Metalworking In Progress
[+]

This armor set is still in active development. Fit, finish, and soft-goods integration are all ongoing.

3D Printing Sewing Aluminum Fabrication Nylon Webbing Vinyl Cutting Airbrush

The B-01 armor is a full wearable costume project balancing shape accuracy, mobility, and layered materials. It combines printed plates, sewn under-structures, metal details, and paint treatment into a final suit that still needs to move like clothing.
