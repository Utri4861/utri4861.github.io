Design: AIM-9 Sidewinder

CAD / Fusion 360

Props & Models Complete
Fusion 360 CAD 3D Printing Tolerance Fitting

A fun exercise on tolerances

Staying on theme and inspired by similar projects, a quick mockup of a missile similar to an AIM-9 was a fun side project.

This project was particularly satisfying in that it was a one-and-done print. No test prints, no reprints — it all worked first time. There was a time I thought I might send it up into the sky, but that time came and went and I ended up giving it to a happy child during a moving sale.
