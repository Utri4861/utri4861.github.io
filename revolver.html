Plans: Colt Walker Revolver

CAD / Fusion 360

Props & Models Complete
Fusion 360 CAD Animation 3D Modeling

Animation

My first foray into Fusion 360's animation abilities, using an early model of the Colt Walker.

Iterations

The sequence in The Good, the Bad, and the Ugly where one of the characters builds his gun from a bunch of components has always stuck with me. I love the idea of customization in general and always love it when video games allow you to swap out parts.

This project became an exploration of that idea — a series of different barrel and cylinder combinations, each with its own character:

  • The "skeleton" of the gun — no barrel assembly
  • An elegant setup — rounded, engraved body with a tapered barrel
  • My personal favorite: a cartridge conversion, inspired by the appearance of the Gasser M1870

Homemade plans found on the web for the Colt Walker proved a challenging Fusion 360 project that taught more than a few lessons.
