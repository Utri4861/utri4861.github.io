About Me

I'm a hands-on maker specializing in cosplay, prop fabrication, and game development. I combine 3D printing, traditional fabrication, and digital design tools to bring detailed projects to life — from screen-accurate costumes to interactive game worlds.

Whether I'm hammering steel for a suit of armor, modeling parts in Fusion 360, or scripting enemy AI in Godot, the throughline is always the same: I love making things.
